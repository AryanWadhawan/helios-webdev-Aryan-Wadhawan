Top 5 Science News of 2022-2023!

1. Sindhuja-1 taps marine energy

Together with IIT-Madras, a startup is trying to turn ocean waves into electricity. Sindhuja-1 is the name of the gadget they developed to facilitate the idea.

2. AI-powered fighter jet

United Kingdom, Italy, and Japan are working together to create a new fighter jet. The jet will incorporate artificial intelligence (AI). The next-generation fighter jet is anticipated to be operational in the mid-2030s.

3. Cancer-free girl thanks to revolutionary treatment

A revolutionary treatment cured a girl of cancer. Medical experts believed that the cancer was incurable. 13-year-old Alyssa was diagnosed with leukaemia.

4.First lab-made human blood test

People took part in the first clinical trial ever to use blood made in a lab. First-time human red blood cells were successfully made in the lab and given in a clinical trial.

EV battery that lasts for 20 years and charges in 3 mins

A new company makes an EV battery that can be fully charged in three minutes and lasts for 20 years. With lithium-metal technology, the battery can be charged and discharged more than 10,000 times over its lifetime.
